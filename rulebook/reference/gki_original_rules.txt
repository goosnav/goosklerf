[BOX] GAMEPLAY → SETUP ↓

(SETUP)

1. CARD DISTRIBUTION:
   CHOOSE OPTION →
   A. SHUFFLE & DISTRIBUTE EQUALLY.
   B. DODGEBALL METHOD: LINE UP ALL CARDS & PLAYERS
   TAKE TURNS CHOOSING EACH CARD.
   C. PLAYERS BRING CUSTOM DECKS.

2. SHOP:
   EACH PLAYER DRAWS 3 CARDS AND
   PLACES THEM IN THE "SHOP" AREA.
   1 CARD MAY BE PURCHASED FOR
   2 CARDS AT THE BEGINING OF A TURN.

3. HAND:
   PLAYERS DRAW 7 CARDS RANDOMLY.

→ TURNS ↓

(TURNS)

1. PLAY:
   USE 3 CARDS IN YOUR HAND.
   → ACTIVATE ENTITY → PLACE FORTRESS
   → EQUIP ENTITY WITH ITEM → BUY FROM SHOP

2. MOVE:
   TRANSFER ITEMS BETWEEN ENTITIES
   AND/OR ENTITIES TO/FROM FORTRESS
   OR BATTLE FIELD

3. ATTACK:
   USING ENTITIES ON BATTLEFIELD,
   ATTACK ENEMIES ON BATTLEFIELD
   AND/OR IN FORTRESSES.

4. DRAW:
   CHOOSE 3 CARDS FROM DECK AND
   ADD TO HAND. THE TURN IS OVER.

[BOX] GAMEPLAY → COMBAT ↓

(Left margin loop)
REPEAT UNTIL RETREAT OR VICTORY

1. ATTACK:  (ATTACKERS GO FIRST)
   i)   EACH ATTACKER SELECTS TARGET.
   ii)  EACH ATTACKER ROLLS 1 DICE (ATTACK OR PARRY)
   — OR —
   • USES AN ITEM
   — OR —
   • USES A SPECIAL* ABILITY

   ```
    NOTE (left-side):
    IF FORTRESS IS DESTROYED,
    ALL DEFENDERS DIE (AFTER BONUS MOVE).

    TARGET CAN BE
    FORTRESS
    OR
    ENEMY
    ENTITY
   ```

   iii) FOR A NORMAL ATTACK, IF THE
   DICE ROLL LANDS ON OR BELOW
   THE ATTACKER’S ATTACK [icon: sword]
   STAT (INCLUDING ITEM BUFFS),
   THE ATTACK HITS & THE TARGET
   LOSES 1 HP. OTHERWISE, IT MISSES.

   iv)  REPEAT FOR ALL ATTACKERS.

2. DEFENSE:
   i)   EACH DEFENDER SELECTS TARGET.
   ii)  EACH DEFENDER ROLLS 1DICE (DEFENSE OR PARRY)
   — OR —
   • USES AN ITEM
   — OR —
   • USES A SPECIAL* ABILITY

   ```
    NOTE (left-side):
    IF A DEFENDER GETS KILLED
    IN THE PREVIOUS ATTACK ROLL,
    IT GETS 1 FINAL BONUS MOVE
    ↓
    UNLESS
    REVIVED,
    DISCARD
    AFTER
    BONUS
    MOVE.
   ```

   iii) FOR A NORMAL DEFENSE, IF THE
   DICE ROLL LANDS ON OR BELOW
   THE DEFENDER’S DEFENSE [icon]
   STAT (INCLUDING ITEM & FORTRESS
   BUFFS), THE DEFENSE HITS AND
   THE TARGET LOSES 1 HP.
   OTHERWISE, IT MISES.

   iv)  REPEAT FOR ALL DEFENDERS.

3. VICTORY:

   * EITHER ATTACKERS RETREAT OR ONE SIDE DIES.
   * IF ATTACKERS WIN, THEY
     COLLECT ENEMY ITEMS.
   * IF ATTACKING FORT, ATTACKERS CAPTURE IT.

[BOX] SPECIAL COMBAT MOVES

o PARRYING:
IF ENTITY IS HIT, INSTEAD OF AN
OFFENSIVE ROLL, ATTACKER/DEFENDER
CAN ROLL AT OR BELOW THEIR
DEFENSE [icon] STAT. IF IT HITS, THE
HP LOSS FROM THE ENEMY INJURY
IS COUNTERED -> THE ENTITY GAINS +1HP.
NOTE: SPECIAL MOVES OR CONSUMABLE
ITEMS CANNOT BE PARRIED.

o RETREAT:
INSTEAD OF ATTACKING, AN ENTITY
CAN LEAVE COMBAT. BEFORE LEAVING,
THE DEFENDERS GET A FINAL CHANCE
TO TARGET & HIT THE ATTACKER.
