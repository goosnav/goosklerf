SINGLEPLAYER:
Setup
1. Sort cards into piles: Fortresses, entities, non-consumable items
2. Shuﬄe each sorted pile, place face down
3. Map: Randomly select and place 3+ fortresses face down on the table (do not look at card)
4. Enemies: Randomly select and place 3 entities with 1 item each face down on each fortress
(do not look at card)
1. First fortress: 1 entity with 1 item
2. Second Fortress: 2 entities with 1 item each
3. Third fortress: 3 entities with 1 item each
4. Fourth fortress: 3 entities with 1 item each
5. Fifth fortress: 3 entities with 2 items each
6. Sixth fortress+: 3 entities with 3 items each
Shuﬄe remaining cards (EXCEPT FORTRESSES)
Hand: Draw 7 cards randomly
Reinforcement Deck: Look through deck and select an additional 5 entities and 10 items
1. Easy: 7 entities & 15 items in deck (3+ fortresses)
2. Medium: 5 entities & 10 items in deck (3+ fortresses)
3. Hard: 3 entities & 10 items in deck (4+ fortresses)
4. Infinite: 7 entities & 15 items in deck (keep adding fortresses and entities on the map
after you defeat one)
Gameplay
1. Using cards in your hand, create a strike force of as many entities as you can, using an
equipping up to 7 cards
2. Attack first fortress on your campaign. Flip over all enemy cards and roll dice for their
attacks. Do not make strategic decisions for enemy (i.e. do not leverage special attacks)
3. After fortress combat, fill up your hand with 7 cards. Play up to 7 cards to modify your
army.
4. Attack the next fortress.
5. Continue until all cards in deck are exhausted, all fortresses are defeated, or all your entities
are killed. Winning condition: You defeat all fortresses. If playing with indefinite fortresses,
score is based on number of fortresses and enemies defeated.
Score:
Super-Fortress = 10 points
Fortress (rare - orange) = 7 points
Fortress (normal - blue) = 5 points
Super-Entity = 5 points
Entity (rare - purple) = 3 points
Entity (normal - red) = 1 points
Player Round —> combat round —> Turn —> Attack/Defense Rolls —> Attack/Defense Roll
MISC RULES:
- This game requires brains, not just brawn. You can only use cards that you understand. If you
are guessing as to what the card means, you must discard it or not use the special. If an enemy
player sees that you do not understand a card’s meaning, he or she can force you to discard it.
