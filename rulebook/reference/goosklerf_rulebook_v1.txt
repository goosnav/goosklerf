ADVANCED:
1. PARRYING
- INSTEAD OF ATTACKING, YOU CAN NEGATE YOUR ENEMY'S LAST ATTACK BY PARRYING. PERFORM AN ATTACK ROLL, AND IF YOU HIT, YOUR ENEMY'S ATTACK IS VOIDED. THIS DOES NOT AFFECT SPECIALS OR CONSUMABLE ITEM EFFECTS. 
2. CARD SHIELD
- FLIPPING EQUIPPED CARDS ON THEIR BACKS TURNS THEM INTO SHIELD CARDS. YOU CANNOT USE THESE CARDS, BUT IF AN ENEMY LANDS AN ATTACK ON YOU, YOU CAN DISCARD THE CARD INSTEAD OF TAKING DAMAGE. EACH CARD = 1HP. 
3. HIGH RISK, HIGH REWARD
- INSTEAD OF YOUR NORMAL ATTACK, YOU CAN ROLL A 1. IF YOU HIT, YOU REMOVE -2 HP FROM YOUR ENEMY, NOT JUST 1HP.
